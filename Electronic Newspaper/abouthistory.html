Our History

Our story began in 2005, when a group of passionate automotive enthusiasts came together to create a platform for luxury car enthusiasts to share their love for high-end automobiles. Our founders had a vision of creating a website that would not only be a source of news and reviews but also a community for like-minded individuals to connect and engage with each other.
Over the years, we have achieved many milestones and grown into one of the most respected voices in the industry. In 2010, we launched our first print magazine, which quickly became a must-read for luxury car enthusiasts around the world. In 2015, we were honored to receive the "Best Automotive Website" award at the prestigious International Automotive Media Awards.
In 2018, we launched our first-ever international event, which brought together car enthusiasts from all over the world for a weekend of driving, networking, and celebrating the best of the automotive world. The event was a huge success, and we have since continued to organize similar events in different parts of the world.
As we look back on our history, we are proud of the many achievements and milestones we have accomplished. From our humble beginnings as a small website, we have grown into a global brand that is synonymous with luxury and high-end automobiles. We are grateful for the support of our readers and fans, and we look forward to continuing to serve as the premier source of luxury automotive news and content.
